How to Make a Claim

Four simple steps from accident to settlement — no confusion, no delays.

1

Call Us Immediately

Dial our 24/7 hotline: 011-123-4567 or WhatsApp us right from the scene.

2

Get Police Report

Visit your nearest police station and obtain a Motor Traffic Accident Report (Form 68).

3

Submit Documents

Send us: completed claim form, police report, driving licence, and photos of damage.

4

Get Repair Approved

We survey the damage and issue a repair order to your chosen garage. Settlement in 7 days.
