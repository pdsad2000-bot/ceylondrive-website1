Policy Details

Everything you need to know about your cover — written in plain, simple English.

What is Covered ✔

CeylonDrive policies provide protection for a wide range of risks that Sri Lankan drivers face every day.

🚗

Own Vehicle Damage

Repair or replacement costs when your vehicle is damaged in a road accident, regardless of who is at fault (Standard & Premium plans).

✔ Standard ✔ Premium
👥

Third-Party Liability

Covers bodily injury and property damage caused to another person by your vehicle. Mandatory by Sri Lankan law. Up to LKR 500,000 for injury.

✔ All Plans
🔥

Fire & Explosion

Vehicle damage resulting from accidental fire, self-ignition, lightning, or explosion. Covers both own vehicle and nearby property (Standard & Premium).

✔ Standard ✔ Premium
🌊

Flood & Natural Perils

Protection against damage from flooding, storms, and other natural disasters. Sri Lanka's monsoon seasons are covered under Standard (partial) and Premium (full).

✔ Standard (partial) ✔ Premium (full)
🔑

Theft & Burglary

Full vehicle theft or break-in damage. We pay the agreed vehicle value (less excess) if your car is not recovered within 60 days (Standard & Premium).

✔ Standard ✔ Premium
🏥

Personal Accident

Lump-sum payment for driver accidental death or permanent disability. Premium plan extends cover to passengers as well.

✔ Standard — LKR 750K ✔ Premium — LKR 1M

What is Not Covered ✘

Understanding exclusions is just as important as knowing what is covered. These are standard industry exclusions.

🚫

Driving Without a Licence

Any claim arising while the driver does not hold a valid Sri Lankan driving licence for that vehicle class will be rejected.

🍺

Drunk or Impaired Driving

Accidents caused while driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs are fully excluded from cover.

🏎️

Racing & Contests

Damage during motor racing, speed trials, or any competitive driving event is not covered under any plan.

⚙️

Mechanical Breakdown

General wear and tear, engine failure, or mechanical breakdown due to poor maintenance is not an insured risk.

📝

Unlawful Use

Using the vehicle for a purpose not stated in the policy (e.g., using a private car for commercial hire) voids the claim.

💻

Pre-Existing Damage

Damage that existed before the policy start date, or damage not reported promptly after the incident, will not be considered.

⚠️ Disclaimer: The coverage and exclusion information above is a plain-English summary for general guidance only. The full terms, conditions, and legal wording are contained in the official CeylonDrive Policy Document. Please request a copy from our team or contact us for clarification before purchasing.
